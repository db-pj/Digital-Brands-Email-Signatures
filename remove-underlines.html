Removing Underlines From Email Signatures

If you have underlines in your email signature, follow these steps:

1. Drag and drop the following link into your bookmarks bar â†’ Remove Underlines

** If you don't have a bookmarks bar, on Windows Chrome you can press Ctrl+Shift+B to make it show up. On Mac Safari, press Command+Shift+B. On Firefox, right click the toolbar and click "Bookmarks Toolbar".

2. Go to Gmail Settings. You should be on the General tab. 3. Click "Remove Underlines" in your bookmarks bar. 4. Scroll to the bottom and click "Save Changes". 5. Send a test email to make sure the underlines are removed. If you're curious, you can view javascript source for the bookmarklet here.
