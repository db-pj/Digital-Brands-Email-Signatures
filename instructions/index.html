Updating Your Digital Brands Email Signature


Update Signature:

• Find your appropriate vertical signature file here.

• With your signature file open: press Ctrl+a then Ctrl+c to select and copy your signature.

• Open up Gmail. Click the Gear Icon near the upper right and click See All Settings.

• On the General Tab, scroll down and find the Signature section. Click + Create New to create a new signature. You can name it "DB Signature" or whatever you like.

• Click inside the Signature text box and press Ctrl+v to paste in your new signature.

• Make sure the "Insert this signature before quoted text in replies..." box is checked.

• Scroll to the bottom and click the Save Changes button.
